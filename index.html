Preventive Recommendations

Regular Exercise

Engage in at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity per week to improve cardiovascular health.

Balanced Diet

Consume a diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins while limiting saturated fats.

Weight Management

Maintain a healthy BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 to reduce strain on your cardiovascular system.

Smoking Cessation

Quit smoking immediately - it's the single most important change for heart disease prevention.

Blood Pressure Control

Regularly monitor blood pressure and maintain it below 120/80 mm/Hg through lifestyle and medication if needed.

Stress Management

Practice stress-reducing techniques like meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises regularly.
